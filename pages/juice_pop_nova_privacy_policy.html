📊 Information We Collect

✅ No personally identifiable information collected

We do not collect any personal data such as name, email address, phone number, or any account credentials. Juice Pop Nova requires no login or registration.

The only data processed is non-personal technical information collected automatically by our advertising partner (Google AdMob) for the purpose of displaying ads:

Device & Technical Information

Device model & manufacturer OS version Screen size & resolution Language & region settings Device configuration

Network Information

Internet connection status Approximate network-based location (non-precise)

Advertising Identifier (AdMob only)

Google Advertising ID (Ad ID) Ad impressions & click data

This data is collected and processed solely by Google. We, the developer, do not store, access, or control this data in any way.

⚙️ How the Information Is Used

The technical data collected by AdMob is used strictly for:

  • Displaying personalized or non-personalized advertisements within the app
  • Measuring ad performance and engagement
  • Preventing fraudulent or abusive ad activity

We do not use any collected data for our own servers, databases, or analytics systems.

🔗 Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated in Juice Pop Nova is:

  • Google AdMob — Displays advertisements; may collect Advertising ID and device info. Google Privacy Policy ↗

We do not use Firebase Analytics, crash reporting tools, social media SDKs, or any other third-party data collection services.

🤝 Data Sharing

✅ We never sell or rent user data

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any user data. We do not have access to any personal user information. Any data handled by Google AdMob is governed entirely by Google's own privacy policy and terms of service.

🛡️ Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data on our end, there is no user data at risk from our side. For information about how Google secures AdMob data, please refer to Google's security documentation.

👶 Children's Privacy

Juice Pop Nova is designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe your child has provided personal data through our app, please contact us immediately at zahirshahin05@gmail.com and we will take appropriate action.
