🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This policy describes how Weather Pro by Shahin Soft handles your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

📍 Location Data

Weather Pro requests device location only to provide local weather forecasts. Location data is:

  • Used in real-time and never stored on our servers
  • Sent securely to OpenWeatherMap API for weather retrieval
  • Never shared with third parties for advertising

You can use the app without location access by manually entering a city name.

🔗 Third-Party APIs

  • OpenWeatherMap — weather data provider
  • Google Firebase Analytics — anonymous usage stats
  • Google Maps SDK — map display (optional feature)
