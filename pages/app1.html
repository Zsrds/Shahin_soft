🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Shahin Soft built Bubble Shooter Nova as a free app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

📊 Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. The app may collect:

  • Anonymous gameplay statistics (level progress, score)
  • Device type and OS version (for crash reporting)
  • App usage analytics via Google Firebase (anonymized)

🍪 Cookies & Tracking

We do not use cookies directly. Third-party services (Google Ads, Firebase Analytics) may use device identifiers for analytics and advertising. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

🔗 Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Google AdMob (for displaying ads)
  • Google Play Services

👶 Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you discover your child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it immediately.
