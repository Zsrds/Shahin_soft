🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Note Keeper by Shahin Soft is designed with privacy first. Your notes are yours — we do not read, sell, or share them.

📝 Your Notes Data

  • Stored locally on your device by default
  • On Firebase (encrypted) only if you enable cloud sync
  • Never accessed or read by Shahin Soft staff

🔐 Security

All synced data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). You may delete your account and all data at any time from app settings.
